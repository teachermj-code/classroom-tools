Mind Your Decisions

The Innovation: A high-engagement review tool that eliminates "spectator downtime" by requiring 100% participation in every round.

1. Universal Challenge: All groups must solve the question within the timer. Everyone reveals their answer simultaneously.

2. Eligibility: Only correct groups receive a +5 Accuracy Bonus and earn the right to enter the Decision Phase.

3. Keep, Give, or Abstain: Eligible groups decide to either Keep mystery points, Give them to all ineligible teams, or Abstain. Choosing to abstain means you only receive the +5 bonus and are protected from other teams' "Give" actions (positive or negative).

Note: Abstaining is the safest strategic move to protect your lead!

Quiz Master Pro

The Innovation: A formal tiered competition engine designed for Summative Assessments and Quiz Bees.

1. Tiered Levels: Organize questions into Easy (1pt), Average (3pts), and Difficult (5pts) categories using the CSV Tier column.

2. Bounty Mode: Press 'B' to toggle Bounty Mode, doubling the score for the current question to increase stakes.

3. Math Fidelity: Fully integrated with KaTeX. Use `$` symbols in your CSV to render professional fractions, roots, and equations.

MCQs Generator

The Innovation: A complete printable worksheet and assessment builder designed to eliminate administrative formatting time.

1. Bulk Import: Paste questions directly from Word/Web or import massive question banks instantly via CSV/Excel.

2. Advanced Shuffling: Randomize just the questions, just the A-D options, or completely shuffle both. The answer key stays flawlessly locked to the correct option no matter what.

3. Print-Ready Output: Generates a clean, distraction-free test paper, a teacher's letter key, and a visual bubble sheet—all perfectly scaled for Long/A4/Letter paper.

Classroom Timer

The Innovation: A precision tool to manage classroom time efficiently, ensuring smooth transitions and focused work periods.

1. Persistent Memory: Remembers your last duration even after a refresh.

2. Visual Cues: High-visibility ring and "Urgency Mode" for the final 10 seconds.

3. Audio Alerts: Distinct procedural ticks and alarm tones.
