Teacher's User Manual

🧠 How to play: Mind Your Decisions

  • 1. Setup: Click the Editor to paste your questions or upload a CSV. Add your class groups (Max 6).
  • 2. The Box: Select a numbered box to reveal a question. Students have 60 seconds to solve it.
  • 3. The Decision: If a group gets it right, they receive a permanent +5 points. Then, they must choose:
    • KEEP: The mystery points in the box are added to their score.
    • GIVE: The mystery points are added to all other teams instead.
  • 4. Strategy: Boxes can contain negative points! Giving away a negative box reduces opponents' scores.

🏆 How to play: Quiz Master Pro

  • 1. Tiered System: Prepare questions for three levels: Easy (1pt), Average (3pts), and Difficult (5pts).
  • 2. Shortcuts: Use Spacebar to start the countdown, 'R' to reveal the answer, and 'N' for the next question.
  • 3. Bounty Mode: Press 'B' to toggle Bonus Points (doubles the score for that round).
  • 4. Math Support: For fractions or roots, use LaTeX code inside $ symbols (e.g., $\frac{1}{2}$).
